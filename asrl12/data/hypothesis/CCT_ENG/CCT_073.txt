The North Green and the Sun were deputing which was the stronger. When the traveler came along wrapped in the warm coat, they agreed that the one who first succeed in marking the travelers take his coat off, should be considered stronger than the other. Then the North Green blew as hard as he could, but the more he blew the more closely the traveler felt his coat around him. And at last, the North Green gave up the attempt. Then the Sun shined out warmly and immediately the traveler took off his coat. And so, the North Green was all pleaked, to confess that the Sun was the stronger of the two.
