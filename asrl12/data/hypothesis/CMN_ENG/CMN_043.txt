The Noswainthe and the Soundward Dispielding, which was stronger, when the Traveller came announced wrapped in a warm clock. The agreed that the one who first succeeded in making the Traveller take his cloak off should be considered stronger and the other. Then, the Noswainthe blew as hard as he could. But the more he blew, the more closely did the Traveller fold his cloak around him. And the Noswainthe gave up as an attempt. Then, the Sound shined out warmly and immediately the Traveller took off his cloak. And so, the Noswainthe was obliged to confess that the Sound was stronger of the two.
